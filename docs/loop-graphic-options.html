Option B · Animated loop

Pros

Cons
  • Diagrammatic — abstract vs. concrete
  • Animation may be distracting for some
  • Doesn't show real Figma/code artifacts

My recommendation: Option A for anyone skimming, Option B if you want motion and have the vertical space, Option C if you can spare the 15 minutes to record. You could also stack them — Option A in the hero as the thesis proof, Option B deeper in the case study where you explain the loop in detail. Mixing is allowed.
